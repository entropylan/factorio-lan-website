Frequently Asked Questions

Is there Internet?⌗

We have a combination of VDSL, Starlink and 5G to provide around 450mbps of bandwidth. We also use LanCache to speed up game downloads.

Is alcohol allowed?⌗

Yes. The venue is not licensed, so you can bring your own alcohol. Please drink responsibly.
