Not been to a LAN party for a while, or want a reminder on what to bring?

## For Your PC

 - PC or Laptop
 - Monitor
 - Mouse
 - Keyboard
 - Headset
 - Mousemat
 - Surge Protected Extension Lead
 - If you're from another country, a UK power adapter (we're 230V)
 - Phone Charger

## For You

 - Food and Snacks
 - Drinks
 - Change of Clothes
 - Campbed/Airbed
 - Sleeping Bag/Duvet

## For Others

 - Shower Gel
 - Towel
