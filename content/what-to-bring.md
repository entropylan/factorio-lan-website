Not been to a LAN party for a while, or want a reminder on what to bring?

 - PC or Laptop
 - Monitor
 - Mouse
 - Keyboard
 - Headset
 - Mousemat
 - Snacks
 - Drinks
 - Surge Protected Extension Lead
 - If you're from another country, a UK power adapter (we're 230V)
